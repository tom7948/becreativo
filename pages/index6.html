BACK TO THE FUTURE
The design style manifests the creepiness and mysterious feeling by adopting the elements of black and white, and especially addresses its double personalities by the color tone.

In the psychological aspect, the association of black links to mystery, unknown and hidden. On the contrary, white  represents innocence, goodness, so as the purest colour. In addition, black and white are complementary colours as main character's  two personalities.
